We'd love you to benefit too. If you're struggling to start here are tips that have really helped us in the past:

  • If you're struggling to set a weekly goal – be realistic. A writing goal can be an amount of words, a length of time or progress on a specific project. Don't be too adventurous but have something to aim at.
  • If you're struggling to set a first step – think tiny! What's the one small thing you can do to move your writing project forward? It doesn't even need to be writing – thinking about a project or reviewing your notes still counts.
