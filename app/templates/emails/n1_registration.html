HOW IT WORKS

We’ve designed it around the science of habits and proven theories of behaviour change for productive writers.

That means – it works. And if you stick with it over just 5 days you will see meaningful progress in your writing – and you’ll be more likely to keep writing in the long term.

  • We won’t tell you what to write – that’s up to you – but we will promise to give you the support, ideas and inspiration you need to start and keep writing.
  • You’ll be asked to track your progress to a deadline and every day we’ll tell you a new secret of writing productivity – backed by science.
  • Because we know life can get in the way of writing we’ve given you two lives to use (in case you need a day off!)
  • But we’re not that nice, you’ll lose a life for each day you miss. If you miss three – you’re off the challenge! No excuses. Sorry :-)
