How it works

We’ve designed this challenge around the science of habits and proven theories of behaviour change for productive writers. That means – it works.

If you stick with it over just 5 days you will see meaningful progress in your writing – and you’ll be more likely to keep writing in the long term. Isn’t that exciting?

  • We won’t tell you what to write – that’s up to you – but we will promise to give you the support and inspiration you need to start and keep writing.
  • You’ll be given a deadline of midnight each night to check in with us and track your progress, and every time you do, we’ll tell you a new secret of writing productivity – backed by science.
  • We give you two lives to use in case you need a day off writing. But you’ll lose a life for each day you miss. If you miss three – you’re off the challenge! Sorry :-)
  • We call this a 5-day writing challenge but if you’re on a roll – you can complete it in less time. We want you to write regularly, we don’t care how fast you write.
