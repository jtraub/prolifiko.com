Now, we’d like to share with you another secret of writing productivity and that’s to…. give yourself regular creative power-ups.

A power-up is a reward or treat that keeps you strong and motivated in your writing. Giving yourself a power-up – a small boost of energy in the right direction – when you’ve completed your task can help you avoid getting side-tracked. Rather than indulging yourself in a procrastination activity before you start, why not book in 30 mins of Facebook time as a power-up for when you’ve done writing for the day? Think of something small enough to encourage, but not too big that writing become all about the power-up, and you’ll find your motivational sweet spot.

If you want to learn more, check out our free resource 5 steps to finding the perfect writing power-up.
