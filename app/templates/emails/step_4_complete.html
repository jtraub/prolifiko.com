I’d like to share with you another secret of writing productivity and that’s to… normalise it.

Science tells us is that if you want to write regularly a great way to do this is to ‘glue’ it to something normal you do every day. For example, make writing the 1st thing you do when you wake up, or after you’ve brushed your teeth or when you have your 1st cup of coffee – then you’re more likely to do it and means it's not such a big deal.
