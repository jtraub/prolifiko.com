About the challenge

This 5 day challenge has been designed around proven theories of behaviour change and the science of habits.

Its aim is to help writers progress a piece of their own writing over 5 days and develop a longer term writing habit.

We don't tell you what to write - that's up to you - but we do promise to give you the support and motivation you need to keep going.

How it works

  1. First, we ask you for your name and email - because we need to keep in contact with you along the way.
  2. Then, you set a writing goal and tell us the first step you'll take towards meeting that goal.
  3. We give you up to 24 hours to complete each step and once you've completed it you 'track' and set the next step. There are 5 steps in total.
  4. Each time you track we email you a new 'secret' of writing productivity - tips backed by science.
  5. Because we're really nice and we know that sometimes life gets in the way, we give you two chances to miss deadline.
  6. But because we're not that nice, you lose a life every time you miss a deadline. Miss two deadlines and you're off the challenge - sorry :-)

About us

Write Track is a startup making digital productivity tools for writers of all types. It was founded by Bec Evans and Chris Smith who have spent many years helping people become more productive writers.

Bec is an expert in habit-forming technology and product innovation and the former director a national writing school. In her spare time she writes short stories and non-fiction.Chris runs a content marketing and digital media consultancy and he's spent a career writing and coaching other people to write. He's also an award-winning scriptwriter of short comedy films.

Together they have written for a whole host of publications like The Guardian, Life Hacker, Startup Grind, The Next Web, Mslexia and The Bookseller - and many more.
